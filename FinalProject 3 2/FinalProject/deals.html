Covent Garden Market

Covent Garden Market is a historic covered market in the heart of London's West End. It's known for its unique shops, street performers, and restaurants. Visitors can find everything from handcrafted jewelry to gourmet food. Normal price: £10. On offer: £7.

London Eye

The London Eye is a giant Ferris wheel located on the South Bank of the River Thames. It offers stunning views of the city from a height of 135 meters. Normal price: £25. On offer: £18.

Madame Tussauds

Madame Tussauds is a wax museum that features lifelike wax figures of celebrities, historical figures, and sports stars. Visitors can take photos with their favorite stars and learn about their achievements. Normal price: £30. On offer: £22.

British Museum

The British Museum is one of the world's oldest and largest museums, featuring a vast collection of art and artifacts from ancient and modern civilizations. Visitors can see the famous Rosetta Stone, the Parthenon sculptures, and many other treasures. Normal price: £18. On offer: £12.

Visit Official Website

Tower of London

The Tower of London is a historic castle located on the north bank of the River Thames. It has served as a royal palace, a prison, and a place of execution. Visitors can see the Crown Jewels, explore the White Tower, and learn about the castle's fascinating history. Normal price: £30. On offer: £20.
